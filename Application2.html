MISSING YOU STUDIOS

Apply to ART - 3D Artist

Sucker Punch isn't currently hiring for a specific role, however we are always interested in seeing inspirational talent.  Even if you don't see a role in your specialty posted, we may be on the hunt in the near future.  Feel free to drop your resume and portfolio here! Successful applicants may meet the following:

  • Portfolio of work demonstrating strong proficiency in your specialty. 
  • Professional experience working on current generation games.
  • Self-driven with strong communication skills.
  • Ability to mentor and teach other artists.
  • Understanding of source control via Perforce or equivalent.
  • Ability to meet deadlines under tight schedules.
  • Five or more years of professional experience.
  • Previous console development experience.

Applicants should expect to take an assessment as a first step of the application. Applicants must be able to work in the Philippines. Most of our programming roles are hybrid or on-site in Pampanga, Angeles City.
